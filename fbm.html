The Problem

First-time travellers like to travel safely and don’t want to plan too much for their trip.

These users want to find memorable places to go and relax and take amazing photos of their experience and share them online.

The Solution

We offer an app that allows users to find locations nearby and filter out the tourist traps.

Foodbyme is curated by online influencers and bloggers, so users know they can rest assured that wherever they decide to go and explore will be an amazing photo-op experience.

Prototyping

The app icon, splash screens and onboarding (through the sign up process) was created through prototyping.

Selecting interests to optimise the generation of recommendations and the dashboard was also created.

Guerilla Testing

Through guerrilla testing, users found navigating the app to be logical and simple.

I found there was a high importance for both the user and Foodbyme to fix the uploading of a profile photo icon as users had failed to previously recognise the correct button to upload a profile photo
