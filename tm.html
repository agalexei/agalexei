Tinder Matchmaker App

Tinder Matchmaker allows the user’s friends to “swipe right” on potential matches to help find the ideal date for the user.

Your friends tend to have a better idea of what you deserve in an ideal partner over yourself, so why not let them pick out a date for you?

Allows you to let your friends set you up for a blind date.

Client
Tinder
Year

Service

The Problem

People feel online dating is very superficial and this is causing our users to get frustrated and ghosted.

The Solution

Tinder Matchmaker is designed to achieve more meaningful connections for our existing users by involving more people in the process and make meeting Tinder matches more fun and interesting.

User Testing Plan & Notes

How intuitive and speedy the onboarding process is for Tinder Matchmaker? Can users intuitively sign up to the app?

How intuitive and easy is it for the user to send link to a friend to play matchmaker? To find out which version (A or B) of sending a link is more intuitive and easier for the matchmaker

How intuitive and easy is it for the matchmaker to message a potential match. Can the matchmaker navigate to messaging with a potential match?

How intuitive and easy is it for the matchmaker unmatch with candidate. Can the matchmaker navigate the unmatching function?

Key Learnings

Re-design the Invite a Friend" feature

Add a Match screen

Change I Agree button to Continue.

Next Steps

Improve the elements on the ‘chats to friends’ screen to incorporate the bios of all the matches your friend can chat for on the user’s behalf.

Creating the ability to vote and poll candidates amongst a group chat of friends.

‘Anti-filter’ AI technology to combat ‘cat-fishing’.
