Tinder Matchmaker

Tinder Matchmaker allows the user’s friends to “swipe right” on potential matches to help find the ideal date for the user.

Your friends tend to have a better idea of what you deserve in an ideal partner over yourself, so why not let them pick out a date for you?

Allows you to let your friends set you up for a blind date.

Year

Part 1

The Problem

Users feel that Government websites like the CER are stale, are hard to navigate and have too much, small text.

The Solution

The CER website was redesigned to make the navigation user friendly, and reduce the information overload.
